Timeline

November 1955

Because of the Current conflict between the two biggest Superpowers (Ussr and Usa) Ideology wars are happening. Like the Korean war between Communist Korea vs Capitalist Korea. One also sparked out in Vietnam.

April 1955

South Vietnam starts losing the war, because of the Communist Nations like Ussr and China providing heavy support to North Vietnam. The seeming victory of the North made some other neighboring countries start their own communist movements; Like Cambodia.

Late January 1968

South Vietnam Allies started being less and less, the United States started giving heavy weaponry and more advanced arms to the South Vietnamese. The South was taking the Offensive now.

January 1969

Even with the heavy Supplying of South Vietnam, the Vietcong tactics still were enough to beat the heavily armed south. This caused the Usa to start doing something called “Vietnamization”; This would be a plan to gradually lower and withdraw troops from South Vietnam.

1975

Laos, another Southeast Asian country that Borders Vietnam, started having their own communist movements, largely influenced by Communist Vietnam winning. The heavy communism in the area made the Us start bombing both Laos and Vietnam to the point where Laos is currently the most heavily bombed country per capita.

April 30th 1975

Saigon, or Modern Ho chi Minh City, was conquered by the Communist North. Labeled as the “Fall of Saigon.” The fall of Saigon was considered something good for the South Vietnamese since the United States and Capitalism was not treating the South correctly. The Fall of Saigon was directed into the Reunification of the Vietnamese and into the New “People’s Republic of Vietnam”.
