History

The vietnam war was a conflict between thirteen countries which was targetted against the French. North Vietnam, back then, fought for its independence. Their goal was to unify the country under a communist government, while South Vietnam was actively fighting to prevent communism from spreading, backed by the United States of America. Deciding on which side was justified during this war is difficult because both sides used violent tactics in order to get what they wanted, like bombings and guerilla fighting.

Affected countries

A lot of countries were affected in this war, and there were many ways in which they got affected.
